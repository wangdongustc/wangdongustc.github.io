3D Biomedical Imaging Device

A combination of VR/AR technology with biomedical devices

Traditional Devices and Technology Trends

Traditional Medical Imaging Device:

2D, Screen-based


+

3D Camera:

3D reconstruction, visible light only


=

3D Imaging Device:

3D, VR/AR based presentation

3D Imaging Device Features

- Precise location information of the target tissue for doctors.
- Liquid crystal switch between dual NIR cameras and dual VIS cameras, so that the 3D reconstruction can be conducted both for NIR information and VIS information.
- Real-time information visible to operation doctors through AR devices, and visible to remote doctors through VR devices.
